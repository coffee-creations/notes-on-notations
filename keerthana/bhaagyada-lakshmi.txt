

Bhagyada Lakshmi baaramma
(O Goddess of prosperity, please come in)
Nammamma nee sowbhagyaada Lakshmi baaramma
(Mother, you are the Goddess of good fortune Lakshmi, please come)

Gejje kaalgaLa dhwaniya tOruta
Hejjeya mEle hejjeya nikkutha
Sajjana sadhu poojeya vElege
Majjige yoLagina benne yanthe

Kanaka vrushTiya kareyuta baare
Manake maanava siddhiya tOre
Dinakara kOTi tEjadi hoLeyuava
Janaka rayana kumaari bEga

Notes:

Ragam Madya mavathi.  

Arohanam:   Sa Ri Ma Pa Ni Sa
Avarohanam:   Sa Ni Pa Ma Ri Sa

For Sa two holes should be covered.
For Ri one hole should be covered.
Ma 6 holes excepting the first and the last like the Ma in Mayamalava Gowla Ragam, ( if you do not remember refer Sarali Varisai series)
For Pa 5 holes.
For Ni 3 holes of the flute should be covered.(Refer Malarnthum Malaratha song. It's Ni is done by covering 3 holes)
